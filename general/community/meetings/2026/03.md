## Agenda {/* #agenda */}

**Topic:** Moodle Design System

**Speakers:** Mathew May (Moodle) and Andrew Gosali (Moodle)

**Quick topic summary:** Moodle's new Design System will both simplify how developers create a correct and consistent LMS user experience, as well as bridging the gap between product experience experts and Moodle development. This talk, presented by the Moodle Design System team, details the plans for the Design System, an overview of how it works, and outlines what is already available to use and test (at the time of the talk).

This was a great topic for us to re-launch these meetings with, because it's a really exciting step forward in Moodle design and frontend development, and will have a big positive impact for all Moodle developers.
